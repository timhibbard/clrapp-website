Privacy policy

Your privacy matters. Christian Radio Finder is designed to keep your listening personal and secure.

  • Location Data: Used only to find nearby stations; not stored or shared with third parties.
  • iCloud Sync (Pro): Synced data stays in your personal iCloud account and is not accessible to the developer.
  • Analytics: No analytics or user tracking data is collected.
  • Streaming: Audio streams connect directly to station servers; listening habits are not tracked.
  • In-App Purchases: Managed by Apple's App Store; developer only receives purchase verification.
  • No Advertising: No ads and no advertising trackers.
  • Data accessed by app: Location (in-use), iCloud (optional Pro), audio playback, reminders (optional permission).
Do I need to enable location services?

Yes, location access while using the app is required for nearby station lookup.
